MOM Talks: Webinar Series on Mechanics of Materials
A series of online talks on the broad theme of "Mechanics of Materials" will be held on Wednesdays in the months of September to December 2020. Times vary according to the location of the speaker, but are mostly scheduled around 9 PM, Indian Standard Time. To convert to your local time you can use this time convertor . These are detailed 45-60 minute talks, delivered by recognised experts in the field and are meant for active researchers in this area. The choice of topics reflects the diverse range of multi-disciplinary fields where advanced mechanics of solids has made significant impact in recent years.

The talks are open and anybody can access them at the dates and times given below. The list is evolving and we will update it as and when we hear from the people we have sent requests to.

All talks may not be archived for later viewing. Please join the talks at the scheduled times. There may be a limitation on the number of participants. Help us spread the word.
